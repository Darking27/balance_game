Balance Game

Tilt your phone and move the red ball into the blue hole. The ball can only enter the blue hole if it is slow enought, otherwise it will jump over the hole. The levels will become harder with each level. Good Luck!

The game is not compatible with this device. Please try again with a mobile device.
